At TwentyTwo we believe a small interdisciplinary team works smarter and faster to solve your business challenges. We love collaborating closely with our clients to develop innovative solutions to business problems, no matter how big. TwentyTwo is focused on two pillars: agency services, and venture services. Within each of these service areas, TwentyTwo provides strategy, research, user experience, design, development, and cloud architecture capabilities to solve a wide array of client needs.

Approach

We begin any engagement with a lean team, and a tailored process to delivery; we believe that each client’s needs are unique, and deserve a solution that fits that need, not a ‘one size fits all’ process. We know that adopting a fully agile methodology may not work in your environment, but waterfall is too slow and antiquated. That’s why we want to work the way that allows us to collaborate and deliver for you. We keep our team small, so that we can move fast, and stay flexible. This means that you work closely with each individual on the team; this helps us do the best work we can, without all the overhead.
